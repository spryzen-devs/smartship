# 🚛 Smart Logistics Platform

An **End-to-End Intelligent Logistics Management System** designed to optimize transportation, improve visibility, and reduce operational costs in modern supply chains.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Logistics in India faces several critical challenges:

- 🚚 60% of freight depends on road transport → inefficient routing  
- 📉 Lack of real-time shipment visibility  
- 🧾 Manual shipment and driver management  
- 💸 High transportation costs (up to 50%)  
- 🧠 No intelligent decision-making systems  

### Key Pain Points

- Unpredictable delivery delays  
- Poor fleet utilization  
- No centralized logistics system  

---

## 💡 Solution

We built a **Smart Logistics Platform** that provides:

- 📦 Shipment Management  
- 🚛 Driver & Fleet Management  
- 🏬 Warehouse & Inventory Control  
- 📍 Real-Time Tracking  
- 🧠 Intelligent Analytics  

---

## 🧠 System Overview

Warehouse → Shipment → Driver & Vehicle → Live Tracking → Analytics

### Explanation

- **Warehouse** → Stores goods  
- **Shipment** → Handles movement of goods  
- **Driver & Vehicle** → Executes delivery  
- **Tracking** → Monitors real-time location  
- **Analytics** → Improves decision-making  

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

### 📦 Shipment Management
- Create and manage shipments  
- Track lifecycle: `Pending → In Transit → Delivered`  

### 🚛 Driver & Fleet Management
- Assign drivers and vehicles  
- Monitor availability and utilization  

### 🏬 Warehouse & Inventory
- Dynamic inventory management  
- Automatic stock updates via shipment lifecycle  

---

## 📍 Real-Time Tracking

- Live truck movement on map  
- Route-based simulation (not random tracking)  
- Track multiple shipments simultaneously  

**Note:**  
Tracking continues even when the user navigates away — powered by backend-driven simulation.

---

## 🧠 Smart Features

### ⏱ ETA Calculation
- Calculates remaining time and distance  

### ⚠ Delay Detection
- Compares expected vs actual delivery time  

### 🔄 Dynamic Status Updates
- Automatically updates shipment status based on movement
